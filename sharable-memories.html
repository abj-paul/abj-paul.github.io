4 PRECIOUS Dream about Zombie Apocalypse

<2021-11-19 Fri>
I had the best dream today. I want to relive it, i want to relive it forever please, And the tragedy is, I am already forgetting the dream. I hate reality and rationality.

The dream might have been influenced by the plot from zombie sister strategy. Me and some zombies had regained their sanity. We were trying to fight something - not the human, some sort of ultimate villain, i did not really dive into that in the dream.
We were in some sort of huge tower, full of mind boggling technology. And the part from where I remember is, I/We had some sort of mission. This mission was of utmost importance but you guessed it - its a suicide mission and I was designated for it - as I had the ability of invisibility.

And now comes the most treasured part that I wish to feel, live inside, forever. I don't know her, I have never seen her face. She was also a awakened super-powered zombie but she was really powerful, almost the most powerful sentient existence in earth. We were together for a long time. And in the suicide mission, I had to go but … maybe she did not want me to. Its not a "maybe". She did not want me to go.
I remember as we were walking down that half-destroyed long tower and she was with me, as if waiting for something. I knew what she was waiting for - just a single word - "No". If I said no, the mission will be cancelled, we can always try something new. But both of us knew that it was almost the most important mission for the civilization, it must not be ignored.
I steeled my heart, We were at the fourth floor or the 2nd floor perhaps. I could not take it anymore, I could feel her shaking. I turned around and sprint towards her and hugged her so tightly. We cried in each other's arms. I felt like the hug was not strong enough so I took my hands under her arms and hugged her tightly(I was slightly shorter than her). And we cried for so long in each other's arm. Sometimes there were sounds, sometimes it was just silent sorrow. I did not ever want to end that moment, I wanted to hug her and stay like that forever, there were some godly unearthly peace in that, I can not explain it. Its as if all my sorrows, emotions, anticipations are being…overflown by pure sense of happiness. No, it was not happiness, it was something else. longing, satisfaction, sorrow of incoming tragedy and a lot of other feelings.
But I, voluntarily ended that long hug and started running. I really wanted to look back and see her one last time but I knew I must not do that. My determination will fly away like a feather If I look at her. I will never want to be away from here.
I kept running, shakily.
Soon I was in front of A.K. High School. I met my mother there, she was here to meet me after doing a ritual in Shani Temple perhaps. I am not sure. I took the prasad from the shop(I am a zombie so the stereotype says I am unholy and should not be able to touch holy things) but I just did that. Meaning sentient zombies are not unholy. Anyway, i did not focus on these details in the dream.
I looked at my mother, I wanted to tell her about my suicide mission and hug her and say her "Goodbye". But I could not, my voice was choking from sorrow, I could not even utter a word.

We crossed the road in front of A.K. High School and started walking towards the unknown. I woke up at that point. Maybe I was able to tell mother, maybe I was not.
Was that mission successful? I do not know.

So much sorrow, sacrifices lay behind a war. They go unnoticed, buried by the murky water of political history. What will my beloved do after my death? Are the rest of my family alive to look after my mother? I do not know. I…

I want to relive that dream, I want to be in her hug forever. I felt heaven in that split seconds of my dream - all my sorrows, fears, emotions were being drowned with that overwhelming heavenly feeling.
They say, Dreams only last for split few seconds, did it? I don't believe so. Maybe I was experiencing another life and she really does exist and all of that, indeed, happened!

I am starting to forget the dream, Reality is a broken mirror shard. It hurts.
